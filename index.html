[Ambigious Utopia]

Ambiguous Utopia (working title) is an music-influenced top-down Action Shooter and Tower Defense game in which players develop and protect their "music core" with different weapons and towers, while many game elements are linked with the background tracks of the game both visually and mechanically.

[Sound]

To make the sounds working in the game, we heavily leverages the new Audio System in Unity 5, Reaktion (Github) and our homebrew audio layering system Hymmnos and runtime Sequencer.

The background music of the game will change dynamically depending on a variety of variables and events, such as the number of towers, number of enemies and whether the player is waiting for respawn or not.

[Colors]

In the game, players can only place towers on hexagon tiles with a Color Tint, and different Color Tint will give the tower different powers.

Strong
Slow
Swift
Splash

The systems expands the types of towers the player can build while keeping a small set of prototype towers to simplify the player controls.
